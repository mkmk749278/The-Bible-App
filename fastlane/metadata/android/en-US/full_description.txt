Manna is a calm, offline-first Bible made for India — for readers and, just as much, for listeners. Open it like morning sunlight entering a church: warm, quiet, and free of clutter, ads, and noise.

WORKS FULLY OFFLINE
Every core feature works with no internet. Five complete Bibles are built in — English (World English Bible) plus Tamil, Hindi, Telugu and Malayalam (Indian Revised Version) — ready the moment you install. Read on a train, in a village, or in airplane mode.

READ OR LISTEN
More than half of us would rather listen than read. Manna reads any chapter aloud with on-device text-to-speech in your Bible's language, and streams human-narrated audio where it's available — with a gentle, automatic fallback so you always hear something. Tap play and let the Word wash over you.

TWO LANGUAGES, INDEPENDENTLY
Your app language and your Bible language are separate. Use a Tamil interface with an English Bible, or a Hindi interface with a Telugu Bible — whatever fits your heart and your household.

ELDER / SIMPLIFIED MODE
For elders and those who cannot read, Simplified Mode makes the text large, the buttons big, and the audio automatic — so opening a chapter simply begins to read it aloud.

PRAYER, WOVEN IN
A quiet Prayers hub holds the Rosary (Japamala) with authentic prayers in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil and Malayalam, the Stations of the Cross, the Jesus Prayer, and more. A liturgical calendar walks you through the year, and a daily verse with a gentle reminder keeps Scripture close.

MAKE IT YOURS
Highlight verses, bookmark them, and keep private notes and a prayer journal — all stored on your device. Share a verse as a beautiful image card for WhatsApp.

UNDERSTAND THE WORD
Tap a verse and choose "Explain this passage" for a short, pastoral explanation — on supported phones this runs privately on-device.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No ads. No tracking. Ever. Your notes, prayers and highlights never leave your phone, and are not copied to cloud backup. Manna connects to the internet only to download Bibles, stream narrated audio, or (optionally) explain a passage — never to collect anything about you.

Manna means "daily bread from heaven." May it be that for you.
