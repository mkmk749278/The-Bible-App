Privacy Policy — Manna

App: com.manna.bible · Last updated: 20 June 2026 · Contact: mulakapati446@gmail.com

Manna is built privacy-first. We designed it so the app is fully useful without an account, without tracking, and without ads — ever.

The short version

  • No account is required to use any core feature.
  • We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
  • No analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs are in the app.
  • Your spiritual data stays on your device — your notes, highlights, bookmarks, prayers, reading position and settings are stored locally and are not uploaded to us or to any cloud service. They are also excluded from Android's automatic Google backup by design.

What data the app handles

Stored only on your device

The following never leaves your phone:

  • Highlights, bookmarks, and notes
  • Prayer journal entries and reflections
  • Reading progress and your last reading position
  • App settings (languages, theme, audio preferences, reminders)
  • Downloaded Bible translations

You can erase all of it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Network requests the app makes

Manna connects to the internet only for these purposes, and only to these hosts:

  • bible.helloao.org — the key-less, MIT-licensed Free Use Bible API, used to download Bible translations and to fetch streamed narrated-audio links. Only generic content references (translation id, book, chapter) are sent. No personal data is sent.
  • generativelanguage.googleapis.com — optional. Used only if you tap "Explain this passage" and a Gemini API key has been configured for the build. In that case the relevant scripture text and a prompt are sent to generate an explanation. No personal data, account, or device identifier is sent. If no key is configured, this feature is disabled and no request is made.

All network traffic uses encrypted HTTPS. Offline-first features (reading bundled Bibles, on-device text-to-speech, notes, prayers, daily verse) work with no network at all.

On-device AI

On supported devices, "Explain this passage" can run on-device via Gemini Nano, in which case nothing is sent off the device.

Permissions

  • Internet / Network state — to download Bibles and stream narrated audio.
  • Notifications — to show the optional daily-verse reminder you choose.
  • Run after restart — to re-arm your daily reminder after the phone reboots.

We request no location, contacts, microphone, camera, or storage permissions.

Children's privacy

Manna does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The app contains no ads and no data collection.
